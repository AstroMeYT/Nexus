How to Use Nexus

  • Write your notes using Markdown formatting.
  • Link concepts together using double brackets: [[Ideas]] or [[Projects]].
  • Switch to the Preview tab to see formatted text and click your links.
  • Switch to the Graph tab to explore your spiderweb map of connections.
  • Ghost nodes (gray) on the graph are pages you've linked to but haven't created yet. Double-click them to automatically create the page.
  • Everything saves automatically to your browser via IndexedDB.

Settings

Export your notes to a JSON file. Importing a file will merge those notes into your existing ones.

About Nexus

Nexus is a local, private knowledge base running entirely in your browser without requiring external servers.
